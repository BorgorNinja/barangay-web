Barangay General Trias


The city has a land area of 90.01 square kilometers or 34.75 square miles which constitutes 5.90% of Cavite's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 450,583. This represented 10.37% of the total population of Cavite province, or 2.78% of the overall population of the CALABARZON region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 5,006 inhabitants per square kilometer or 12,966 inhabitants per square mile.

The City of General Trias

Originally named after its Patron Saint, Francis of Assisi, 1181-1226, the municipality of General Trias was founded in December 13, 1748. The original name of the town was changed to General Trias in honor of Gen. Mariano Trias (1869-1914), by virtue of a law fathered by Emilio P. Virata during his term as representative of Cavite, 1919-1921.
